Highly Energetic

During the last five academic years, 100+ activities have taken place at our university. These include presentations, hands-on workshops, hackathons, guest lectures, meetups, open days, excursions and even barbeques!

02
Community Powered

The community is our key strength. 20+ individuals contribute their time, talent and skills to the advancement of their favourite projects. All the magic happens within our unique 60m2 space. We are well-equiped and ready to innovate!

03
Open-Source Doers

Our work gets noticed worldwide! Apart from local activities, we contribute to famous upstream projects such as Mozilla, Wikipedia and Fedora. Our members have participated at 50+ conferences held in 20+ different countries.

About fossaegean

This enthusiastic group focuses on diverse topics such as cybersecurity, privacy, Linux, artificial intelligence, and gaming, among others.
